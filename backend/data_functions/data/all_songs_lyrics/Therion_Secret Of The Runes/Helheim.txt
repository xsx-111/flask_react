Helheim LyricsHel, the godess of death is also the godess of fulfilment. Her name means both "hole" and "whole" and she governs not only death but also the process of birth and rebirth. Hell was hurled down to the world of ice in the beginning of time and there she created nine dark worlds. The bright god Balder and the power of the sun descend into her misty realm, bu in the future Balder will be reborn and rise from the womb of Hel like the sun in the springtime
